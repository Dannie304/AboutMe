MENTOR INFORMATION

MrBeast, whose real name is Jimmy Donaldson, is a popular American YouTuber, philanthropist, and entrepreneur. He was born on May 7, 1998, in Kansas, USA. He rose to fame on YouTube for his viral stunts, challenges, and philanthropic acts. MrBeast started his YouTube channel in 2012 and initially created videos about gaming. However, he soon switched to creating videos about stunts, challenges, and charitable acts. His philanthropic acts include donating millions of dollars to various charitable causes and organizations, such as planting 20 million trees, helping homeless people, and supporting food banks. His YouTube channel, MrBeast, has over 72 million subscribers, making it one of the most popular channels on the platform. He has also launched his own merchandise line, MrBeast Burger, which is available in select locations across the United States. MrBeast has received numerous accolades for his work, including being named one of Time magazine's 100 most influential people in the world in 2020. He continues to create engaging content on YouTube and use his platform to make a positive impact on the world.
